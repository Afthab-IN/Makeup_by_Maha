Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 6-8 months in advance, especially for peak wedding seasons, to secure your preferred date.

Do you travel to venues?

Yes, we are based in Chennai but open to work anywhere globally. Please note that travel and stay arrangements must be taken care of by the client.

What brands do you use?

We use only premium and luxury HD products including MAC, Charlotte Tilbury, NARS, Huda Beauty, and Dior.

Do you do trial sessions?

Absolutely. We highly recommend a trial session 1-2 months before the wedding to lock in your perfect look.
